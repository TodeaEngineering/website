
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Kubernetes 1.36 a safe upgrade for production clusters?

For most teams, yes. Version 1.36 brings together stable, mature features. Before you upgrade, check your manifests for `gitRepo` (which has been removed) and `service.spec.externalIPs`.

### Do I have to migrate off `service.spec.externalIPs` immediately?

No, you do not need to migrate right away. Removal is planned for v1.43, but it is best to start now. Migrate to a LoadBalancer Service or a Gateway API resource.

### How does DRA AdminAccess moving to Stable affect GPU and AI platforms?

You can now make privileged debugging or inspection claims on shared GPUs by setting `adminAccess: true` on a `ResourceClaim`. Allowed only in namespaces with the admin-access label.
