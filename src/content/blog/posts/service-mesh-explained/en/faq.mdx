
## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does a service mesh actually do?

Handles mTLS, retries, timeouts, identity, and metrics via platform-managed proxies, so developers don’t reimplement these features.

### Do I need a service mesh?

Yes, for consistent mTLS, routing, rate limiting, and observability across services, especially with mixed languages or third-party apps.

### Does a service mesh help with compliance like PIPA, GDPR, or PCI DSS?

Yes. A service mesh enforces mTLS between services, helping meet encryption requirements without app code changes.
