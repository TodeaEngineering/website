
## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is Linkerd different from Istio?

Linkerd is small, fast, and operationally boring. Istio is broader and pluggable. Pick Linkerd to stay out of the way, Istio when you need extensibility.

### Does Linkerd encrypt service-to-service traffic by default?

Yes. Once a workload is meshed, all TCP traffic between pods is encrypted with mTLS, using short-lived workload certificates issued and rotated automatically every 24 hours.

### Is Linkerd or Istio easier to debug?

Linkerd is easier to debug with a simple proxy and fixed admin interface. Istio enables deep Envoy tracing but is more complex. Ambient simplifies L4, but L7 still depends on Envoy.
